Pagani Zonda Cinque

The Pagani Zonda Cinque is one of the rarest and most extreme hypercars ever produced. Limited to only **five units**, the Cinque was developed as the ultimate version of the Zonda, blending performance from the Zonda R with street legality.


Designed using advanced carbon-titanium materials, the Cinque achieves incredible rigidity while remaining extremely lightweight. It features unique styling elements such as the signature roof air scoop, massive rear wing, and exposed carbon fiber bodywork.


Powered by a tuned Mercedes-AMG V12, the Zonda Cinque delivers brutal acceleration, razor-sharp handling, and a spine-tingling exhaust note. Today, it is one of the most desirable and valuable hypercars in Pagani’s history.

Engine

  • Power: 678 hp (505 kW)
  • Torque: 780 Nm
  • Inconel & titanium exhaust system

  • Paddle shifters

Performance

  • 0–200 km/h: 9.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~350 km/h
  • Downforce optimized aerodynamics
Chassis & Suspension
  • Carbon-titanium monocoque chassis

  • Extremely lightweight construction
Brakes

  • Ventilated discs
  • Track-level stopping performance
Dimensions
