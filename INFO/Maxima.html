Nissan Maxima

The Nissan Maxima is a full-size sedan known for blending luxury, performance, and advanced technology. Often called the “four-door sports car,” the Maxima combines sleek styling, a powerful V6 engine, and a comfortable interior, making it a popular choice for drivers who want performance without sacrificing practicality. Since its introduction in 1981, the Maxima has evolved into a technologically advanced and stylish vehicle.

The latest Maxima comes with a 3.5 L V6 engine producing strong horsepower and torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) for smooth acceleration and fuel efficiency. It offers a suite of advanced driver-assist features, such as ProPILOT Assist, adaptive cruise control, and blind-spot monitoring. With spacious seating and premium interior materials, the Maxima rivals luxury sedans while remaining more affordable.

Today, the Nissan Maxima continues to deliver a balance of performance, comfort, and technology, making it a competitive choice in the full-size sedan segment. Its combination of sporty handling, luxury features, and everyday usability ensures that it remains a relevant and appealing option for drivers.

Engine
  • 3.5 L V6 (VQ35DE)
  • Power: 300 hp
  • Torque: 353 Nm
Transmission & Drivetrain
  • Xtronic CVT
  • Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: ~5.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~250 km/h
  • Fuel Economy: ~9–10 L/100 km
Chassis & Suspension
  • Front: Independent strut suspension
  • Rear: Multi-link independent suspension
  • Sport-tuned suspension (higher trims)
Brakes
  • Ventilated disc brakes (front & rear)
  • ABS, EBD, stability control
Dimensions
  • Length: ~4,915 mm
  • Width: ~1,850 mm
  • Height: ~1,440 mm
  • Wheelbase: ~2,775 mm
  • Weight: ~1,660–1,720 kg
Interior & Practicality
  • 5 seats
  • Premium leather options
  • Touchscreen infotainment
  • Trunk space: ~450 liters
