Pagani Utopia

The Pagani Utopia is the third hypercar from Pagani Automobili, following the legendary Zonda and Huayra. Unveiled in 2022, it represents Horacio Pagani’s vision of purity and simplicity—combining analog emotion with state-of-the-art engineering.

It is powered by a bespoke Mercedes-AMG 6.0-liter twin-turbo V12 producing 864 hp and 1100 Nm of torque. Buyers can choose between a 7-speed Xtrac manual gearbox or an automated manual, making the Utopia one of the only modern hypercars to offer a true manual option.

Limited to just 99 units worldwide, the Utopia stands as one of the last pure, unfiltered combustion hypercars — crafted with Pagani’s signature artistry in metal, carbon, and titanium.
