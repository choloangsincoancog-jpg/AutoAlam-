Ford Escape

The Ford Escape is a compact SUV designed to combine efficiency, practicality, and modern technology. First introduced in 2000, the Escape has evolved through multiple generations, offering a variety of powertrains, including turbocharged 3-cylinder engines, naturally aspirated 4-cylinders, and hybrid variants. Its compact dimensions make it ideal for urban driving, while still providing spacious seating and cargo capacity for small families or active lifestyles.

The Escape offers features such as Ford’s Co-Pilot360 safety suite, SYNC infotainment system, and advanced driver-assist technology. With optional all-wheel drive, the Escape can handle a variety of road conditions, from city streets to light off-road trails. Its combination of fuel efficiency, comfort, and technology makes it a competitive choice in the compact SUV market.

The modern Ford Escape continues to prioritize versatility and practicality, offering configurable seating, hybrid options for improved fuel economy, and a user-friendly interior design. It remains a popular choice for buyers seeking a compact SUV that balances performance, technology, and daily usability.

Engine Options
  • 1.5 L EcoBoost Inline-3: 181 hp, 257 Nm torque
  • 2.0 L EcoBoost Inline-4: 250 hp, 373 Nm torque
  • 2.5 L Hybrid I4: 200 hp, 210 Nm torque (combined)
  • 2.5 L Plug-in Hybrid: 221 hp combined
Transmission & Drivetrain
  • 8-Speed Automatic Transmission (gas engines)
  • eCVT (hybrid)
  • Front-Wheel Drive (standard)
  • Intelligent All-Wheel Drive (optional)
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: ~7.0–8.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~180–200 km/h
  • Fuel Economy: 5.5–8.0 L/100 km
Chassis & Suspension
  • Independent front and rear suspension
  • Electric power-assisted steering
  • Optional adaptive damping system (higher trims)
Brakes
  • Four-wheel disc brakes
  • ABS and stability control standard
Dimensions
  • Length: ~4,613 mm
  • Width: ~1,884 mm
  • Height: ~1,684 mm
  • Wheelbase: ~2,710 mm
  • Weight: ~1,600–1,900 kg
Interior & Practicality
  • Five-seat configuration
  • Digital instrument cluster
  • SYNC infotainment system
  • Split-fold rear seats
  • Cargo space: ~908 liters (seats folded)
