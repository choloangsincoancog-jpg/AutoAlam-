Ferrari Daytona SP3

The Ferrari Daytona SP3 is a limited-production masterpiece from Ferrari’s **Icona Series**, inspired by the legendary 1967 1-2-3 victory at the Daytona 24 Hours. With only **599 units** produced, it blends retro racing heritage with modern hypercar technology.


Using a naturally aspirated V12 mounted in a mid-rear layout, the SP3 features one of Ferrari’s most powerful engines ever — derived from the 812 Competizione and tuned for more power and response.


Its sculpted body, targa-style roof, unique rear louvers, and flowing aerodynamic surfaces create a design that is both futuristic and heavily inspired by classic Ferrari prototypes. The Daytona SP3 stands as one of Ferrari’s most beautiful and emotional creations.

Engine
  • 6.5 L Naturally Aspirated V12 (F140HC)
  • Power: 840 hp (618 kW)
  • Torque: 697 Nm
  • Redline: 9,500 rpm
  • Dry sump lubrication & titanium connecting rods
Transmission
  • 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT)
  • Ultra-fast shifting derived from 812 Competizione
  • Rear-wheel drive
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: 2.85 seconds
  • 0–200 km/h: 7.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~340 km/h
  • One of the fastest naturally aspirated Ferraris ever made
Chassis & Suspension
  • Full carbon-fiber chassis (F1-derived)
  • Targa top design
  • Active aerodynamics with passive efficiency
  • Double wishbone suspension with adaptive dampers
Brakes
  • Brembo carbon-ceramic discs
  • Track-focused brake cooling
  • High-performance ABS/EBD system
Dimensions
  • Length: 4,686 mm
  • Width: 2,058 mm
  • Height: 1,142 mm
  • Dry Weight: 1,485 kg
Interior & Features
  • Carbon-fiber racing cockpit
  • Fixed seats with adjustable pedals
  • Driver-focused minimalist design
  • Latest Ferrari Side-Slip Control (SSC 6.0)
