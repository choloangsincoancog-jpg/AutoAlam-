Pagani Zonda Barchetta

The Pagani Zonda Barchetta, unveiled in 2017, represents the ultimate evolution of the legendary Zonda line. Created to celebrate Horacio Pagani’s 60th birthday, the Barchetta features extreme performance, exclusive craftsmanship, and a unique open-roof design.


Its exposed wheels, shortened windshield, and ultra-lightweight carbon-titanium monocoque make it one of the rarest and most distinctive hypercars ever built. With only **3 units** produced, the Zonda Barchetta stands among the most exclusive vehicles on the planet.


Powered by a naturally aspirated AMG V12, paired with exquisite Pagani artistry, the Barchetta is a masterpiece that blends engineering excellence with automotive art—making it a prized treasure for collectors worldwide.

Engine
  • 7.3 L Mercedes-AMG M120 V12 (Naturally Aspirated)
  • Power: 789 hp (588 kW)
  • Torque: 813 Nm
  • Redline: 7,500 rpm
Transmission
  • 6-speed manual
  • Rear-wheel drive
  • Triple-disc clutch
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: ~3.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 355 km/h
  • One of the fastest naturally aspirated hypercars
Chassis & Suspension
  • Carbon-titanium monocoque
  • Front & Rear: Double wishbone with Ohlins adjustable dampers
  • Ultra-light chassis for maximum rigidity
Brakes
  • Brembo Carbon-Ceramic brakes
  • Ventilated discs
  • Extreme lightweight braking system
Dimensions
  • Length: 4,390 mm
  • Width: 2,055 mm
  • Height: 1,145 mm
  • Weight: 1,250 kg
Interior & Features
  • Hand-crafted leather & aluminum interior
  • Exposed carbon fiber structures
  • Analog performance gauges
  • Bespoke Italian artisanal design
