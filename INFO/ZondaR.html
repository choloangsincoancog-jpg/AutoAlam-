Pagani Zonda R

The Pagani Zonda R is a track-only hypercar built with no racing restrictions, allowing Pagani to push engineering to an extreme. Released in 2009, it became known for its ultra-light structure, radical aerodynamics, and razor-sharp driving dynamics.

Power comes from a naturally aspirated 6.0-liter AMG V12 producing 750 hp, paired with a lightning-fast 6-speed sequential gearbox. The result is a machine designed purely for speed, precision, and raw racing intensity.

With only 15 units produced, the Zonda R remains one of the rarest and most iconic track weapons ever created.

Engine
  • 6.0 L AMG V12 (NA)
  • 750 PS • 710 Nm
  • Dry sump lubrication
  • Derived from CLK-GTR
Transmission
  • 6-speed sequential
  • Paddle-shift
  • Rear-wheel drive
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: ~2.7 s
  • Top Speed: ~350 km/h
  • Nürburgring: 6:47
Chassis
  • Carbon-Titanium monocoque
  • Double wishbone suspension
  • Öhlins racing dampers
Brakes
  • Brembo carbon-ceramic
  • Ventilated discs
Dimensions
  • Dry Weight: ~1,070 kg
  • Length: 4,886 mm
  • Width: 2,014 mm
  • Height: 1,114 mm
