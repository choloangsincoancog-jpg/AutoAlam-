Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is one of the most iconic American muscle cars in automotive history, first introduced in 1964. As Ford’s flagship sports car, the Mustang redefined performance and style in its era, becoming a symbol of freedom, power, and rebellion. Over the decades, it has evolved through multiple generations, combining modern engineering with the classic muscle car spirit that enthusiasts love.

The Mustang offers a variety of powertrains, from turbocharged 4-cylinder engines to naturally aspirated V8s, allowing it to cater to both daily drivers and high-performance enthusiasts. Its aggressive styling, long hood, and short rear deck give it a distinctive look that has become instantly recognizable worldwide. High-performance variants such as the Mustang GT, Shelby GT350, and GT500 push the car to supercar levels of acceleration and handling, while retaining everyday usability and a strong connection to its classic heritage.

Engine Options
  • 2.3 L EcoBoost Inline-4 Turbo: 310–330 hp, 350 Nm torque
  • 5.0 L Coyote V8 (GT): 450–480 hp, 570 Nm torque
  • 5.2 L V8 (Shelby GT350 / GT500): 526–760 hp, 582–847 Nm torque
Transmission & Drivetrain
  • 6-Speed Manual Transmission (GT / Shelby)
  • 10-Speed Automatic Transmission (EcoBoost / GT)
  • Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.5–5.5 seconds (varies by engine)
  • Top Speed: 240–320 km/h depending on variant
  • Quarter Mile: ~11–12 seconds (GT / Shelby)
Chassis & Suspension
  • Independent rear suspension (all modern models)
  • MacPherson strut front suspension
  • Performance packages with MagneRide adaptive dampers (GT / Shelby)
Brakes
  • Disc brakes all around
  • Optional Brembo brakes (GT / Shelby)
  • ABS and stability control systems
Dimensions
  • Length: ~4,788 mm
  • Width: ~1,916 mm
  • Height: ~1,382 mm
  • Wheelbase: ~2,720 mm
  • Weight: 1,600–1,900 kg depending on variant
Interior & Practicality
  • 2+2 seating layout
  • Digital instrument cluster and infotainment system
  • Premium leather and optional Recaro sport seats
  • Front and rear cargo space suitable for daily use
