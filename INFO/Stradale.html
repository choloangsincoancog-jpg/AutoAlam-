Ferrari SF90 Stradale

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale is Ferrari’s first-ever **plug-in hybrid** supercar, combining a twin-turbo V8 engine with three electric motors to deliver unprecedented performance.


Named after Ferrari’s 90th anniversary, the SF90 represents a new era of electrified performance, offering Formula 1–inspired technology, advanced aerodynamics, and a completely redesigned interior focused on digital driver interfaces.


With nearly **1,000 horsepower**, all-wheel drive, and instant hybrid torque, the SF90 Stradale accelerates faster than many hypercars — making it one of Ferrari’s most advanced and powerful road-legal machines ever built.

Engine
  • 4.0 L Twin-Turbocharged V8
  • Three electric motors (one rear, two front)
  • Combined Power: **986 hp (1,000 CV)**
  • Torque: 800 Nm+
  • Hybrid plug-in system with 7.9 kWh battery
Transmission
  • 8-speed dual-clutch automatic (DCT)
  • All-wheel drive (first Ferrari V8 AWD)
  • Electric-only driving mode (up to 25 km)
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: **2.5 seconds**
  • 0–200 km/h: **6.7 seconds**
  • Top Speed: **340+ km/h**
  • Lap Time (Fiorano): 1:19.0
  • Active aerodynamics with electronic control
Chassis & Suspension
  • Lightweight aluminum & carbon-fiber hybrid chassis
  • Magnetorheological suspension (SCM-E)
  • High-downforce aerodynamic package
Brakes
  • Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Electric brake-by-wire system
  • High-performance cooling design
Dimensions
  • Length: 4,710 mm
  • Width: 1,972 mm
  • Height: 1,186 mm
  • Weight: 1,570 kg (dry: 1,570 kg)
Interior & Features
  • 16-inch curved digital cockpit display
  • F1-style steering wheel with touch controls
  • Premium carbon fiber & leather interior
  • Hybrid system driving modes (eDrive, Hybrid, Performance, Qualify)
