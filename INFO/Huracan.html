Lamborghini Huracan

The Lamborghini Huracán represents a significant evolution in Lamborghini’s lineup, succeeding the Gallardo as the brand’s entry-level V10 supercar. Introduced in 2014, the Huracán blends extreme performance with advanced technology, refined aerodynamics, and Lamborghini’s unmistakable aggressive design language. Its name is inspired by a famous Spanish fighting bull, “Huracán,” symbolizing strength, courage, and ferocity.

Powering the Huracán is a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine, delivering around 610 to 640 horsepower depending on the variant. The engine is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, offering lightning-fast gear changes and exceptional driving dynamics. The Huracán is available in rear-wheel-drive and all-wheel-drive configurations, providing both thrilling agility and confident handling at extreme speeds. Variants like the Performante, EVO, and STO pushed the car further, adding enhanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and track-focused performance without sacrificing daily usability.

Engine
  • 5.2 L Naturally Aspirated V10
  • Power: 610 PS (Huracán LP610-4)
  • Power (Performante / EVO): 640 PS
  • Torque: ~560–600 Nm
  • Redline: ~8,500 rpm
Transmission & Drivetrain
  • 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (LDF)
  • All-Wheel Drive (LP610-4 & Performante)
  • Rear-Wheel Drive (LP580-2, STO)
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: 2.9–3.2 seconds (depending on variant)
  • Top Speed: ~325 km/h
  • Drive Modes: Strada, Sport, Corsa, EGO
Chassis & Suspension
  • Aluminium and carbon-fiber monocoque chassis
  • Magnetorheological suspension (on EVO & Performante)
  • Pushrod suspension system (Performante & STO)
Dimensions
  • Length: ~4,520 mm
  • Width: ~1,933 mm
  • Height: ~1,165 mm
  • Wheelbase: ~2,620 mm
  • Weight: ~1,422–1,542 kg (depending on variant)
Interior & Practicality
  • 2-seat cockpit
  • Digital instrument cluster
  • Infotainment system with navigation and Apple CarPlay
  • Premium leather and Alcantara trim
  • Small front luggage compartment (~100 liters)
