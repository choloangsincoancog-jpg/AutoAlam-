Lamborghini Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is Lamborghini’s first high-performance SUV, launched in 2018. It combines aggressive design, exotic engineering, and supercar-level performance in a practical 5-seat package.

Powered by a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 with up to 666 PS (Performante), the Urus reaches 0–100 km/h in 3.3–3.6 seconds. All-wheel drive, adaptive suspension, and multiple drive modes make it capable on road and off-road.

The Urus became Lamborghini’s best-selling model, defining the “Super SUV” category while keeping the brand’s emotion, style, and performance intact.

Engine
  • 4.0 L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Power: 650 PS (641 hp)
  • Performante: 666 PS
  • Torque: 850 Nm
  • Redline: ~6,800 rpm
Performance
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.6 s (Performante: 3.3 s)
  • Top Speed: 305 km/h
  • Drive Modes: Strada, Sport, Corsa, Sabbia, Terra, Neve
Transmission & Drivetrain
  • 8-Speed Automatic
  • All-Wheel Drive
  • Active Torque Vectoring
  • Rear-Wheel Steering
Chassis & Suspension
  • Adaptive Air Suspension
  • Active Anti-Roll Bars
  • Carbon-Ceramic Brakes
  • Aluminum & High-Strength Steel Frame
Dimensions & Interior
  • Length: 5,112 mm | Width: 2,016 mm | Height: 1,638 mm
  • Wheelbase: 3,003 mm | Weight: ~2,150 kg
  • 5-seat configuration | Premium interior | Cargo: 616 L
